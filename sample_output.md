### Cultural Tips
- Bow slightly when greeting or thanking someone
- Remove shoes when entering homes, some ryokans, and traditional restaurants
- Queue on the left side of escalators (right lane kept clear for walkers)
- Cash is still preferred at many small restaurants, shrines, and markets
- Voice calls on trains and subways are considered rude – keep phone on silent
- Tipping is NOT customary in Japan – it can even cause offence
